Ground to ground coldframe greenhouse

EGS 16' and 20' ground to ground Coldframes are one of the strongest ground to ground structures on the market. An easy build, and cost effective way to get you covered. These structures can be customized to meet your growing needs. Add fans and heaters to optimize the growing potential of these structures.

Ground to ground coldframe with sidewalls

An ideal option for the urban farmer, or ambitious home gardener, EGS coldframes are the next step in expanding your growing options. Available in a 3' 6" tall, or optional 6' tall, straight sidewall height, this house can be employed for many different growing conditions. Available in 20' and 24' widths. Combine this structure with our full line of Greenhouse accessories to make this a year round production house.
