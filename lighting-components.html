Lighting Components

Power Supply Cords

These power cords are compatible with all ballasts that feature Smart Volt power cords. This includes Sun System 1, 6, & Crop Master, Harvest Pro, Harvest Pro Elite, Budget Gro II, Galaxy and Lumatek.

Lamp Extension Cords

These cords plug into the female lamp cord receptacle on a Sun System® ballast then into the male plug on the lamp cord coming from the reflector. Allows a user to increase the distance between the ballast and reflector by 10'. Match cord gauge to your existing lamp cord gauge.

Fluorescent and HID bulb sockets

Flexible “pigtail” strain relief. 5kv mogul base socket. Aluminum socket cap. 8’ power cord with socket for use with mogul based self-ballasted compact fluorescent lamps. 15’ lamp cord with socket for use with all mogul based HPS & MH lamps (ballast required).

AVAILABLE FOR REMOTE HPS BALLAST REBUILDS. 250/400/430W -- 400W -- 600W -- 1000W. Major brand and imports available at separate costs.

Capacitors

These major brand capacitors are rated for 100° or 105° C temperature. The higher temperature rating means long lasting durability.

T5 H.O. Replacement Ballasts

Reliable, high output ballasts. Easy installation. Instant start ballasts. Use shunted sockets. 36” leads.

T5 ReplacementDigital Ballast

Major brand electronic ballast with a small profile for easy installation. Easy Push-Lock wire systems. Requires grounded power cord. All ballasts are program start. Use non-shunted sockets. 50” leads.

Socket Extenders - NOT FOR 1000W USE!

These socket extenders are used to lengthen an existing socket when its position is fixed in an HID lighting reflector. Most modern reflectors are optimized for longer 1000 watt lamps. This allows a user to better position a 250 to 600 watt lamp in these reflectors. For use with 250-600 watt lamps. Do NOT use with 1000 watt lamps. 4KV pulse rated. Extends socket 2-3/8”.

Universal Socket Assembly

Our universal socket assembly is compatible with all of our reflectors and both MH & HPS lamps. We go the extra mile by offering 5KV/horizontal position oriented mogul base sockets. White powder coated junction box and cover. Includes strain relief, but not lamp cord.
