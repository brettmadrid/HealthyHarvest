Welcome to Healthy Harvest South

In April 2012 we opened Healthy Harvest South in Keizer, OR. We have a very comfortable shopping atmosphere meeting the needs of the Keizer/Salem gardeners.

A Grow Shop for Everyone!

Whether your growing in soils, soilless mixes, hydration grow rocks, coco fiber or rockwool we offer everything needed to be a successful gardener. Everything from grow tents to lighting and everything in between - we've got you covered!

Did you say Hydroponics?

We offer a complete line of hydroponic supplies to grow with any method of hydroponics including DWC & RDWC Systems (Recirculating DWC), Ebb and flow (flood and drain),Top Feed And drain to waste,Aquaponics,Aeroponics.

Grow Media

If you are looking for grow media Grodan Rockwool,Mother Earth Perlite,Coco Coir,Hydroton clay pellets and many other types of media we got you covered.

Who We Are

Norther Oregon's premier hydroponic store

We opened our doors in Hillsboro on April, 15th 2011, launching our first Healthy Harvest Gardening store. Our goal is to carry top of the line hydroponic and indoor/outdoor gardening supplies along with competitive pricing in a comfortable atmosphere. According to our customers - we have succeeded!
